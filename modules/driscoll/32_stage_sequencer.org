#+TITLE: 32 Steps Sequencer


more than a 32 steps sequencer, this module is also a bidirectional 32 stage multiplexer.

as such it can be seen like a mix bewteen the sequencer (SEQ) and the bi-directional router (ROU).

the [[https://www.nonlinearcircuits.com/modules/p/32to1][NLC 32:1]] is the result of this work.
